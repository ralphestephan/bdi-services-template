## Risk Management & Quality Control

### Link Quality Assurance
- **Domain Authority Verification**: Minimum DR 35 for all target sites
- **Spam Score Monitoring**: Reject domains with spam score >3%
- **Content Relevance**: Ensure all backlinks from business/technology contexts
- **Geographic Relevance**: Prioritize MENA-focused or global business sites

### Penalty Prevention
- **Natural Link Velocity**: Gradual backlink acquisition pace
- **Anchor Text Diversity**: Varied anchor text to avoid over-optimization
- **Link Diversity**: Mix of guest posts, directories, partnerships, and earned links
- **Regular Audits**: Monthly backlink profile reviews and disavowal when needed
